ABOUT BSMC

Established in the year 1964, Baroda Steel & Metal Corporation is a top player in the category of Steel Dealers in Vadodara. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Vadodara. Over the course of its journey, this business has established a firm foothold in it’s industry. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as their products and services, have helped this establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day.
